TOYP UNIBEN's Impact on the University of Benin Community

TOYP UNIBEN honors young individuals who exemplify the values of leadership, humanitarianism, and community service. This recognition is a celebration of those who have made significant contributions to society, irrespective of their membership in JCIN UNIBEN. By spotlighting outstanding individuals, we motivate others to take action, pursue excellence, and positively impact the world around them. The program continues to inspire young people to lead with purpose and create meaningful change.

Message From Our President

Welcome to the Ten Outstanding Young Persons (TOYP) Award, University of Benin.

On behalf of Junior Chamber International Nigeria (JCIN), UNIBEN, I warmly welcome every student of the University of Benin to this prestigious platform that celebrates excellence, impact, and purposeful leadership.

At its core, TOYP exists to encourage UNIBEN students to keep thriving and excelling academically, professionally, socially, and in service to humanity. We believe that excellence deserves to be seen, celebrated, and rewarded.

Most importantly, participation in the TOYP Award is completely free of charge. No application fees. No nomination fees. No hidden payments. We are committed to recognizing true excellence based solely on merit, integrity, and impact.

If you believe in growth, excellence, and the power of young people to shape the future, then you are welcome here. Once again, welcome to TOYP UNIBEN, where excellence is celebrated and potential is amplified.

— JCIN CLLR. IGE EMMANUEL
46TH JCIN UNIBEN LOCAL ORGANIZATION PRESIDENT

Message From Our TOYP Director

Welcome to the official platform of the JCIN UNIBEN Ten Outstanding Young Persons (TOYP) Awards.

As we embark on the 2026 cycle, our mission is clear. While we celebrate youth in general, our specific mandate this year is to elevate the young undergraduate leaders within the University of Benin. We believe that the university environment is not just a place for learning, but a testing ground for the leaders who will shape our nation's future.

The TOYP Awards are designed to recognize substance over popularity. We are looking for undergraduates who are doing more than just passing through the university system; we are looking for those who are actively improving it.

The categories featured on this site, ranging from academic achievement to humanitarian service, reflect the diverse ways our students are driving progress.

I invite you to explore the nominees and the criteria. If you know an undergraduate leader whose efforts deserve a larger platform,or if you are that leader, I encourage you to submit a nomination. Let us recognize the student leaders of today who are already acting as the visionaries of tomorrow.

— Sakpere Montel
2026 TOYP Director | JCIN UNIBEN
