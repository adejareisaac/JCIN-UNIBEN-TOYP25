TOYP UNIBEN's Impact on the University of Benin Community

TOYP UNIBEN honors young individuals who exemplify the values of leadership, humanitarianism, and community service. This recognition is a celebration of those who have made significant contributions to society, irrespective of their membership in JCIN UNIBEN. By spotlighting outstanding individuals, we motivate others to take action, pursue excellence, and positively impact the world around them. The program continues to inspire young people to lead with purpose and create meaningful change.

"The Ten Outstanding Young Persons, UNIBEN has the potential to nurture and foster a passion for excellence in the student community. JCIN UNIBEN is fast becoming one of the most influential forces on campus. Getting to win a TOYP Award from JCIN UNIBEN will drive a passion for excellence within the student community"

Ewere Praise Festus – 2025 JCIN UNIBEN LOCAL ORGANIZATION PRESIDENT

"TOYP UNIBEN has become a catalyst for excellence within the university of Benin Community – spotlighting young changemakers, inspiring innovation, and encouraging students to pursue leadership, entrepreneurship, and community impact. it's more than recognition; it's a movement that drives growth, motivation, and transformation across campus."

Abiodun Taiye Peace – 2025 JCIN UNIBEN LOCAL ORGANIZATION SECRETARY

"TOYP UNIBEN encourages students to think beyound academic achievement and stive for real-world relevance. It inspires young people to challenge the status quo, create solutions to societal problems, and step into roles that shape the future. More than an award. TOYP UNIBEN is a call to action – a driving force that motivates, nurtures, and sustains a campus-wide commitment to excellence, service, and transformation. By spotlighting young changemakers across diverse fields, it ignites a culture of innovation, leadership, and proactive citizenship among the student body."
