TOYP UNIBEN's Impact on the University of Benin Community

TOYP UNIBEN honors young individuals who exemplify the values of leadership, humanitarianism, and community service. This recognition is a celebration of those who have made significant contributions to society, irrespective of their membership in JCIN UNIBEN. By spotlighting outstanding individuals, we motivate others to take action, pursue excellence, and positively impact the world around them. The program continues to inspire young people to lead with purpose and create meaningful change.

Message From Our TOYP Director

Welcome, University of Benin students, staff, and faculty, to the official platform for the Ten Outstanding Young Persons (TOYP) Awards by JCIN UNIBEN!

The TOYP Award is more than a title—it’s a powerful recognition of vision, resilience, and impact by individuals under 40 driving positive change.

UNIBEN is home to countless inspiring students and young professionals making strides in academics, entrepreneurship, community service, and the arts. This award honors their dedication and builds a legacy of excellence.

Know someone outstanding? Or maybe you’re making a difference yourself? Use this platform to nominate yourself or someone who truly embodies impactful leadership.

Let’s celebrate and uplift the next generation of changemakers from the University of Benin!

— Deborah Oluwaferanmi Obelawo
2025 Director of Ten Outstanding Young Persons Award, UNIBEN for JCINUNIBEN
